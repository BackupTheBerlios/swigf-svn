SWIGF Swing Graphical Framework

SWIGF provides a framework to implement graphical editors with shapes and connectors. At the moment it is pre alpha and only a small part of the planned features are implemented. Features are / will be

  • selecting and dragging of shapes
  • snapping shapes and connectors to grids and snappoints of the shapes
  • copying of the graphs into clipboard
